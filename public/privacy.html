Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

VoiceMailAssist ("the App") is a voice-first email and messaging assistant that allows users to control their Gmail and Telegram accounts entirely by voice. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your Gmail email address
  • Your name (from Google profile)
  • OAuth access tokens to read and send emails on your behalf

We also collect activity logs for security and admin monitoring purposes, including login times, emails sent, and Telegram messages sent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Display your Gmail emails in the app
  • Send emails on your behalf when you confirm by voice
  • Provide AI-generated reply suggestions
  • Maintain security logs for the admin dashboard

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not:

  • Store your email contents permanently
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Store your Google password

4. Google OAuth & Gmail API

VoiceMailAssist uses the Google OAuth 2.0 protocol to access your Gmail account. We request the following permissions:

  • gmail.readonly — to read your emails
  • gmail.send — to send emails on your behalf
  • userinfo.email — to identify your account
  • userinfo.profile — to display your name

Your Google OAuth token is stored in your browser session and expires automatically. We comply with Google's API Services User Data Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Session-based authentication with 60-minute timeout
  • 4-digit PIN verification for all outgoing messages
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No permanent storage of email content

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Sign out at any time to end your session
  • Revoke app access at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Request deletion of your account data by contacting us
